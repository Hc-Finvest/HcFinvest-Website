Privacy and Security Policy

Hc Finvest (Heddge Capitals Wealth Management LLC)

1. Introduction

At Hc Finvest (Heddge Capitals Wealth Management LLC), we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy, security, and transparency in all our operations. As a financial and trading-focused platform, our primary goal is to empower traders, investors, and partners to achieve their financial objectives and long-term ambitions with confidence.

We understand that trust is the foundation of any successful financial relationship. Therefore, we prioritize safeguarding your personal information, financial data, and transactional activities through robust security measures and strict data protection practices. Every system, process, and interaction within Hc Finvest (Heddge Capitals Wealth Management LLC) is designed to ensure confidentiality, integrity, and reliability.

Hc Finvest (Heddge Capitals Wealth Management LLC) offers a comprehensive ecosystem that combines advanced financial tools, competitive trading conditions, and a seamless user experience. Whether you are an individual trader or a business partner, we strive to provide dynamic solutions that support growth, performance, and strategic decision-making.

Our mission is to create a secure and efficient environment where users can focus on achieving their financial goals without concerns about data privacy or system reliability. By continuously enhancing our technologies and adhering to global best practices in data protection, Hc Finvest (Heddge Capitals Wealth Management LLC) ensures that your information remains protected at every level.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of information:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Residential address

2.2 Financial Information

  • Bank account details
  • Payment transaction data
  • Trading account activity

2.3 Identity Verification (KYC)

  • Government-issued ID (PAN, Aadhaar, Passport, etc.)
  • Proof of address
  • Verification documents required under regulatory compliance

2.4 Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Device type and browser
  • Login activity and timestamps
  • Cookies and tracking data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • Account registration and management
  • Identity verification (KYC/AML compliance)
  • Executing trades and financial transactions
  • Risk management and fraud prevention
  • Improving platform performance and user experience
  • Customer support and communication
  • Legal and regulatory compliance

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your information with:

  • Liquidity providers and trading partners for order execution
  • Payment processors and banking partners for deposits/withdrawals
  • Regulatory authorities where required by law
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, security tools)

All third parties are obligated to maintain strict confidentiality and data protection standards.

5. Data Security Measures

Hc Finvest (Heddge Capitals Wealth Management LLC) implements industry-standard security measures, including:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Role-based access control
  • Continuous monitoring and threat detection
  • Regular system audits and updates

Despite our efforts, no system is completely immune to risks; however, we continuously strengthen our defenses.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enhance user experience
  • Analyze platform performance
  • Store user preferences
  • Track trading and browsing behavior

Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

7. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Requests can be made through our support channels.

8. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary for:

  • Providing services
  • Meeting legal and regulatory obligations
  • Resolving disputes

After this period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate with third-party services (e.g., trading APIs, analytics tools). These services operate under their own privacy policies, and Hc Finvest (Heddge Capitals Wealth Management LLC) is not responsible for their practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

Hc Finvest (Heddge Capitals Wealth Management LLC) reserves the right to update this Privacy and Security Policy at any time. Changes will be communicated through our website or official communication channels.
